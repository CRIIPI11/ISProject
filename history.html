About The Creator

Since the eraly years, our creator started developing a passion for visual arts. During the early school years Eric started deleping a love for animated visual. Not much long, this love later made Eric start creating his own work such as drawings and ideas of even making a movie one day. As the years passed, Eric started working more making videos for local projects even being asked to produce educational videos for the school at his hometown. Improving his skills Eric later started working on bigger projects for more artists and companies until making what today is The Prime Prodigy Company. However, Eric doesn't plan to stop here as his future plans include making movies of his own and also making a reality all his child characters.
